## ⭐ Featured Project

# SR Hunter V4

**SR Hunter V4** is a layered Pine Script indicator designed for TradingView.

Instead of generating blind buy or sell signals, SR Hunter helps traders evaluate market conditions through a structured decision-making process.

> **Think First. Trade Later.**

SR Hunter is designed as a **trading validation partner**, helping traders understand *why* a setup is valid before making a decision.

---

## 🧠 Core Philosophy

Trading is not about predicting the future.

It is about understanding probability, managing risk, and making disciplined decisions.

SR Hunter follows this philosophy by transforming market analysis into a structured workflow rather than a simple signal generator.

```text
No Blind Signal
No Random Entry
No Emotional Decision

Only Structured Validation
```

---

## ⚙️ Analysis Pipeline

SR Hunter V4 uses a step-by-step validation pipeline:

```text
Trend
   ↓
Structure
   ↓
Location
   ↓
Trigger
   ↓
Quality
   ↓
Risk
   ↓
Decision
   ↓
UI Output
```

Each layer validates the market condition before moving to the next stage.

If one layer fails, the system does not force a trade.

## 🧩 Engine Breakdown

### 📈 1. Trend Engine

The Trend Engine identifies the current market direction using EMA-based logic.

It helps determine whether the market condition is:

- Bullish
- Bearish
- Sideways

---

### 🧱 2. Structure Engine

The Structure Engine reads swing behavior and market structure.

It focuses on price structure such as:

- Higher High
- Higher Low
- Lower High
- Lower Low
- Mixed Structure

---

### 📍 3. Location Engine

The Location Engine checks whether price is near important zones.

It focuses on:

- Support area
- Resistance area
- Near support
- Near resistance
- Neutral zone

---

### ⚡ 4. Trigger Engine

The Trigger Engine looks for possible confirmation behavior.

Trigger examples:

- Breakout
- Pullback
- Break and Retest
- Rejection

---

### 📊 5. Quality Engine

The Quality Engine filters market condition quality.

It may consider:

- RSI condition
- ATR volatility
- Trading session
- Spread proxy
- Market environment

---

### 🛡️ 6. Risk Engine

The Risk Engine checks whether the potential setup has acceptable risk.

It focuses on:

- Risk reward
- Stop distance
- Volatility condition
- Spread condition
- Risk limitation

---

### 🧠 7. Decision Engine

The Decision Engine combines all validation layers and produces final output.

Possible decision states:

```text
BUY
SELL
WAIT
NO TRADE
```

---

### 🖥️ 8. UI Output Panel

The UI Output Panel displays the result directly on the chart.

It is designed to be simple, readable, and useful while keeping the chart clean.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open **TradingView**
2. Go to **Pine Editor**
3. Open the file:

```text
sr-hunter-v4.pine
```

4. Copy the source code
5. Paste it into TradingView Pine Editor
6. Click **Save**
7. Add the indicator to your chart
8. Use the panel as a validation tool
